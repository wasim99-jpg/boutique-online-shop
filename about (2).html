WHO WE ARE





SHINSHI is a great online fashion destination specifically designed for men. We are a fashion-focused e-commerce site based in Malaysia and was founded in late 2020. Having a vision to mainstream modest fashion, we strive to keep the site fresh and always on-trend.



With minimalism, practicality, and comfort as the forefront of our philosophy, we aim to help people all around to curate a well-thought and efficient wardrobe that allows them to be the most creative, the most confident, and the best version of themselves.



Best of all, our products are handcrafted by Malaysians in Malaysia. Support “Made in Malaysia”, we strive to be a national pride as we continue to grow and expand onto an International platform.
